1) Prepare and bake corn bread according to package directions, using the egg and milk.
2) Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
3) Stir in the spaghetti sauce, corn, chilies and sloppy joe mix.
4) Bring to a boil.
5) Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
6) Sprinkle with cheese; cover and cook for 1 minute or until cheese is melted.
7) Cut corn bread into six pieces; cut each piece in half.
8) Top with sloppy joe mixture.
