1) In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 2 tablespoons butter.
2) Add halibut, garlic and thyme.
3) Cook until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 10 minutes.
4) Remove and set aside.
5) Add the remaining butter to the skillet.
6) Stir in flour and salt until smooth; cook and stir until golden brown.
7) Gradually add broth and cream.
8) Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
9) In a greased 13-in baking dish, layer half of the noodles, halibut, white sauce and cheese.
10) Repeat layers.
11) Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes.
12) Uncover; bake 20 minutes longer or until bubbly.
13) Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
14) Sprinkle with parsley if desired.
