1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2) Grease one 9x5 inch loaf pan.
3) Measure flour, 1 cup sugar, baking powder, baking soda, 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon, salt, buttermilk, oil, eggs and vanilla into large mixing bowl.
4) Beat 3 minutes.
5) Pour into prepared loaf pan.
6) Smooth top.
7) Combine 2 tablespoons white sugar, 1 teaspoon cinnamon and butter, mixing until crumbly.
8) Sprinkle topping over smoothed batter.
9) Using knife, cut in a light swirling motion to give a marbled effect.
10) Bake for about 50 minutes.
11) Test with toothpick.
12) When inserted it should come out clean.
13) Remove bread from pan to rack to cool.
