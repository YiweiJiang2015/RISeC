1) Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2) Refrigerate 2/3 of the bacon until needed.
3) Wrap each cocktail wiener with a piece of bacon and secure with a toothpick.
4) Place on a large baking sheet.
5) Sprinkle brown sugar generously over all.
6) Bake for 40 minutes in the preheated oven, until the sugar is bubbly.
7) To serve, place the wieners in a slow cooker and keep on the low heat.
