1) In a large bowl, stir together the flour, 1/2 cup sugar, baking powder, salt, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon and nutmeg.
2) Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, butter, and vanilla.
3) Mix until well blended.
4) Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.
5) Heat oil in a deep heavy skillet or deep-fryer to 370 degrees F (185 degrees C).
6) On a floured board, roll chilled dough out to 1/2 inch thickness.
7) Use a 3 inch round cutter to cut out doughnuts.
8) Use a smaller cutter to cut holes from center.
9) Fry doughnuts in hot oil until golden brown, turning once.
10) Remove from oil to drain on paper plates.
11) Combine the remaining 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon and 1/2 cup sugar in a large resealable bag.
12) Place a few warm donuts into the bag at a time, seal and shake to coat.
