1) Place the beans in a saucepan with enough water to cover them by one inch.
2) Bring beans to a slow boil.
3) Reduce heat to low, and simmer until tender but not mushy (about 35 minutes).
4) Set aside to cool.
5) When the beans have cooled, place them in a large bowl.
6) Add the celery, carrots, parsley, white onion, and mint; toss to blend.
7) For dressing, blend olive oil, orange and lemon juice, garlic, coriander, salt, pepper, and brown sugar in a blender for 6 seconds.
8) Transfer to a small saucepan, and simmer over low heat for five minutes.
9) Remove from heat, and cool.
10) Pour dressing over the bean salad, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
