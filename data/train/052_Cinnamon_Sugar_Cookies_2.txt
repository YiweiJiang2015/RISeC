1) In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter, sugars and oil.
2) Add eggs and vanilla; mix well.
3) Add flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon and cream of tartar.
4) Stir in the pecans if desired.
5) Roll into 1-in.
6) Place on greased baking sheets; flatten with the bottom of a glass dipped in sugar.
7) Sprinkle with colored sugar if desired.
8) Bake at 375 degrees F for 10-12 minutes.
