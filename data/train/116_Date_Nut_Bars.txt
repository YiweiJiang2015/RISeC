1) In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, baking powder, salt, dates and nuts.
2) Add the eggs, butter and vanilla; stir just until dry ingredients are moistened (batter will be very stiff).
3) Spread in a greased 15-in pan.
4) Bake at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
5) Cool bars on a wire rack.
6) Dust with confectioners' sugar.
