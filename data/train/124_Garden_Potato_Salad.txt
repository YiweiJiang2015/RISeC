1) In a large bowl, combine potatoes, eggs, celery, green onions, radishes, salt and pepper; set aside.
2) For dressing, combine eggs, vinegar, sugar, dry mustard and salt in a saucepan.
3) Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened.
4) Cool.
5) Stir in mayonnaise; mix well.
6) Pour over potato mixture; toss to coat.
7) Refrigerate for several hours.
