1) Prepare Fast Chicken Soup Base.
2) Bring to a simmer.
3) Add these: 1 9-ounce package refrigerated small cheese tortellini; 1 14.5-ounce can diced tomatoes; 2 medium zucchini, diced; 1 tsp. dried basil.
4) Then simmer for 10-20 minutes until tender.
5) Before removing from heat, stir in 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley.
6) Add salt and pepper, to taste.
