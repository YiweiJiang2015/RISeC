1) For batter, combine the eggs, water, flour and salt in a blender.
2) Cover and process until smooth; let stand for 15 minutes.
3) Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, saute onion in butter until tender.
4) Stir in flour until blended.
5) Gradually stir in milk.
6) Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until slightly thickened.
7) Reduce heat to low.
8) Stir in 1/2 cup cheese, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, pepper and salt until cheese is melted.
9) Stir in broccoli.
10) Cover; keep warm.
11) Heat a lightly greased 8-in. nonstick skillet; pour 2 tablespoons batter into the center of skillet.
12) Lift and tilt pan to evenly coat bottom.
13) Cook until top appears dry; turn and cook 15-20 seconds longer.
14) Remove to a wire rack.
15) Repeat with remaining batter, greasing skillet as needed.
16) Spoon about 1/2 cup filling down the center of each crepe; roll up.
17) Place seam side down in an ungreased 11-in. baking dish.
18) Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
19) Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 5-7 minutes or until cheese is melted.
