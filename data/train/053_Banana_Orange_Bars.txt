1) In a mixing bowl, beat bananas, sugar, oil and eggs until well blended.
2) Combine dry ingredients; fold into the banana mixture until well mixed.
3) Spread over bars.
4) Pour into a greased 15-in. baking pan.
5) Bake at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes; cool.
6) For frosting, cream butter and sugar in a mixing bowl.
7) Add orange juice and peel and beat until smooth.
