1) In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine water, 2 1/2 tablespoons sugar, salt and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil.
2) Bring to a boil and remove from heat.
3) Stir in flour until mixture forms a ball.
4) Heat oil for frying in deep-fryer or deep skillet to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
5) Pipe strips of dough into hot oil using a pastry bag.
6) Fry until golden; drain on paper towels.
7) Combine 1/2 cup sugar and cinnamon.
8) Roll drained churros in cinnamon and sugar mixture.
