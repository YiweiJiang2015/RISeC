1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2) Score roast on top and sides, and place in a medium sized oven bag.
3) Combine water, soup mix, and Worcestershire sauce; pour over roast.
4) Arrange vegetables around meat.
5) Place oven bag on a roasting pan.
6) Place roast in oven, and immediately reduce heat to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
7) Bake for 1 1/2 hours for medium well done, or adjust time for desired doneness.
