1) Heat oil in a large pot; brown chicken.
2) Remove chicken and set aside.
3) Saute onions until soft, about 5 minutes.
4) Add broth, beans, tomatoes, celery, carrots, wine, bay leaf, herbs and all the chicken; cover and simmer for 40 minutes.
5) Add parsley, cilantro, salt, and pepper, and simmer 5 more minutes.
