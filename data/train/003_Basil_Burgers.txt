1) Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
2) In a bowl, mix the ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, basil, garlic salt, and pepper.
3) Form the mixture into 4 burger patties.
4) Lightly oil the grill grate, and cook burgers about 6 minutes, turning once, to an internal temperature of 160 degrees F (70 degrees C), or to desired doneness.
5) Serve on hamburger buns.
