1) In a bowl, cut margarine into flour until crumbly.
2) Press into an 11-in baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray.
3) Bake at 350 degrees F for 15-18 minutes or until lightly browned.
4) Cool completely.
5) In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sweetener until smooth.
6) Fold in half of the whipped topping.
7) Carefully spread over the crust.
8) In a mixing bowl, combine milk and pudding mix.
9) Beat on low speed for 2 minutes.
10) Let stand for 2-3 minutes.
11) Spread over the cream cheese.
12) Top with the remaining whipped topping.
