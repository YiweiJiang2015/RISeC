1) Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2) Wash beets, and place in a roasting pan.
3) Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until tender. Set aside to cool.
4) Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
5) Stir in onion, pears, sugar, and vinegar; cook, stirring frequently, for 20 minutes.
6) When the beets are cool enough to handle, peel and coursely chop.
7) Puree onion mixture in a food processor with metal blade.
8) Add salt and 1/2 of the beets; pulse 4 to 5 times.
9) Add remaining beets, and pulse 2 to 3 times.
