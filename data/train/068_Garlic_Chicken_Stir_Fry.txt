1) Heat peanut oil in a wok or large skillet.
2) When oil begins to smoke, quickly stir in 2 cloves minced garlic, ginger root, green onions and salt.
3) Stir fry until onion becomes translucent, about 2 minutes.
4) Add chicken and stir until opaque, about 3 minutes.
5) Add sweet onions, cabbage, bell pepper, peas and 1/2 cup of the broth/water and cover.
6) In a small bowl, mix the remaining 1/2 cup broth/water, soy sauce, sugar and cornstarch.
7) Add sauce mixture to wok/skillet and stir until chicken and vegetables are coated with the thickened sauce.
8) Serve immediately, over hot rice if desired.
