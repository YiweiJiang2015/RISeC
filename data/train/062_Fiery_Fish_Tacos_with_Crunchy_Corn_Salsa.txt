1) Preheat grill for high heat.
2) In a medium bowl, mix together corn, red onion, jicama, red bell pepper, and cilantro.
3) Stir in lime juice and zest.
4) In a small bowl, combine cayenne pepper, ground black pepper, and salt.
5) Brush each fillet with olive oil, and sprinkle with spices.
6) Arrange fillets on grill grate, and cook for 3 minutes per side.
7) For each fiery fish taco, top two corn tortillas with fish, sour cream, and corn salsa.
