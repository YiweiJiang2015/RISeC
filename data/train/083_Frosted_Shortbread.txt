1) In a mixing bowl, cream butter and brown sugar.
2) Add egg yolk and vanilla; mix well.
3) Combine flour and salt; add to creamed mixture.
4) Press into a greased 15-in.
5) Bake at 350 degrees F for 15-18 minutes or until golden brown.
6) Immediately place candy bar pieces over crust.
7) Let stand for 1 minute or until softened; spread chocolate evenly.
8) Sprinkle with walnuts.
9) Cool.
10) Cut into squares.
