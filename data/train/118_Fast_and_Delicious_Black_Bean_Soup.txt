1) Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2) Stir in the onion, bell pepper, carrot, and garlic, and cook 5 minutes, until tender.
3) Mix in 1 can black beans and chicken broth.
4) In a blender, puree remaining can of beans until smooth.
5) Mix into the pot.
6) Bring soup to a boil.
7) Reduce heat to low.
8) Mix in ham, cumin, salt, and pepper.
9) Simmer 20 minutes.
10) Garnish with Cheddar cheese and sour cream to serve.
