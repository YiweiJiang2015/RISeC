1) In a mixing bowl, cream butter and confectioners' sugar.
2) Add vanilla.
3) Gradually add flour; mix well.
4) With lightly floured hands, shape tablespoonfuls of batter into cookies.
5) Place cookies apart on ungreased baking sheets.
6) Bake at 350 degrees F for 9-11 minutes or until edges and bottom are lightly browned.
7) Cool for 2-3 minutes before removing to wire racks.
8) In a microwave-safe bowl, melt chocolate chips and shortening; stir until smooth.
9) Drizzle chocolate over half of the cookies.
10) Dip one end of remaining cookies into chocolate, then sprinkle with walnuts.
