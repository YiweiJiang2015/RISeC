1) Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2) In a large bowl, combine bread and milk, and set aside to soak for 5 minutes.
3) Then stir in egg, butter, raisins, mixed fruit, brown sugar, nutmeg, and cinnamon.
4) Mix well.
5) Press the mixture into a  9-inch square baking pan.
6) Bake in preheated oven until golden and firm to touch, about 35 minutes.
7) Leave in baking pan to cool, then cut into squares.
