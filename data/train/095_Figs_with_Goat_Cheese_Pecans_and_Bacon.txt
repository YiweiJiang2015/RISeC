1) Preheat the broiler.
2) Stuff fig halves with goat cheese.
3) Press pecans into the cheese.
4) Wrap each stuffed fig half with half a slice of bacon, securing with toothpicks.
5) Arrange on a medium baking sheet.
6) Broil 5 minutes, or until bacon is evenly brown and crisp and goat cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
