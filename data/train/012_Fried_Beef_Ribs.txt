1) Heat the oil in a deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2) In a medium bowl, mix together the salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, garlic salt, and onion salt.
3) Place the flour in a separate bowl or plate.
4) Whisk eggs in a large bowl.
5) Rub the spice mixture onto all of the ribs.
6) Dip each rib in egg, then in flour, then egg, then flour again.
7) Place the ribs in a fryer basket so they are not crowded, try 4 or 5 at a time depending on the size of your fryer.
8) Cook for about 8 minutes.
