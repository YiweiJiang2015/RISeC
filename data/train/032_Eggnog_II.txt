1) In a large saucepan, combine the eggs, milk and 1/3 cup sugar.
2) Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture coats a metal spoon.
3) Remove from heat.
4) Cool quickly by placing pan in a sink or bowl of ice water and stirring 1 to 2 minutes.
5) Stir in the vanilla.
6) Chill 4 to 24 hours.
7) Before serving, whip the cream and 2 tablespoons sugar until soft peaks form.
8) Transfer chilled egg mixture to a punch bowl.
9) Sprinkle each serving with nutmeg.
10) Fold in whipped cream mixture and serve at once.
