1) In a medium bowl, stir together the oats, flour, baking soda and brown sugar.
2) Cut in the shortening until the mixture resembles cornmeal.
3) There should be no lumps of shortening left.
4) Measure 1/2 cup of the mixture into re-sealable bags or containers for storage.
5) Label and date each bag.
6) To make the cookies, empty one package of the mix into a bowl.
7) Stir in 2 teaspoons of water, 1 tablespoon of raisins and 1 tablespoon of mini semisweet chocolate chips.
8) Mix well and shape teaspoonfuls of dough into small balls.
9) Place balls on an ungreased cookie sheet and flatten with the buttered bottom of a glass dipped in sugar.
10) Follow you oven's instructions for baking.
