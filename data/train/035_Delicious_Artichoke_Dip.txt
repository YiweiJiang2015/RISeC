1) Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2) In a medium-sized mixing bowl, stir together mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan cheese and onion.
3) When these ingredients are combined, mix in artichoke hearts, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
4) Transfer mixture to a shallow baking dish.
5) Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 20 minutes, or until light brown on top.
