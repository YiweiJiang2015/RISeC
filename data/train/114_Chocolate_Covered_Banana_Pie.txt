1) Combine crushed cookie crumbs, pecans, and butter or margarine.
2) Press into a 9 x 13 inch pan.
3) Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 5 minutes.
4) Cool.
5) Combine chocolate chips, 1 cup milk, and 2 cups marshmallows in a sauce pan.
6) Stir over low heat until melted.
7) Pour over cooled crust.
8) Chill for 1 hour.
9) Arrange banana slices over chilled chocolate layer.
10) Prepare pudding mix as directed on box except use only 3 cups of milk.
11) Fold in nondairy whipped topping and remaining marshmallows.
12) Pour pudding mixture over bananas.
13) Grate chocolate bar over the top.
14) Chill for 2 to 3 hours before serving.
