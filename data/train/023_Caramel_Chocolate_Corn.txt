1) Grease cookie sheets.
2) Place popcorn and peanuts in a paper bag.
3) Set aside.
4) In a microwave safe bowl, combine sugar, butter, corn syrup, vanilla and salt.
5) Microwave 4 minutes, until bubbly.
6) Stir in baking soda.
7) Pour over popcorn mixture and shake bag to coat.
8) Roll down edges of bag and place bag in microwave.
9) Heat for 2 minutes, shaking three times to mix.
10) Spread mixture onto prepared sheets to cool.
11) In a microwave safe bowl, combine chocolate and shortening.
12) Microwave 90 seconds, stirring once, until melted.
13) Drizzle over popcorn mixture.
14) Let cool before serving.
