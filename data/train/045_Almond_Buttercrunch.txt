1) In a saucepan over low heat, combine the butter, sugar and 1 cup chopped almonds.
2) Stir constantly until mixture boils, then do not stir or shake.
3) Heat to 300 to 310 degrees F (149 to 154 degrees C), or until a small amount of syrup dropped into cold water forms hard, brittle threads.
4) Pour onto a large buttered cookie sheet.
5) Immediately press chocolate chips onto the hot candy. As the chocolate melts, spread it into a coating over the candy.
6) Sprinkle finely chopped almonds over the top; cool, and break into uneven pieces.
