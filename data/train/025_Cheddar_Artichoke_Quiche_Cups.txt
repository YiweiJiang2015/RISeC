1) Drain artichokes, reserving half of the marinade.
2) Chop artichokes; set aside.
3) In a skillet, saute onion and garlic in reserved marinade until tender; set aside.
4) In a large bowl, combine the egg, bread crumbs, mustard, oregano, pepper and hot pepper sauce.
5) Stir in the cheese, parsley, reserved artichokes and onion mixture.
6) Fill miniature muffin cups three-fourths full.
7) Bake at 325 degrees F for 15-17 minutes or until set.
8) Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pan to wire racks.
9) Serve warm.
