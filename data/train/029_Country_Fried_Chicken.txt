1) In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the first five ingredients.
2) In a shallow bowl, beat the egg and milk.
3) Add chicken to bag, a few pieces at a time, and shake to coat.
4) Dip into egg mixture, then return to flour mixture and shake again.
5) Remove from bag and let stand for 5 minutes.
6) Heat oil in a large skillet.
7) Fry chicken until golden brown on all sides.
8) Reduce heat to medium and cook until a meat thermometer reads 180 deg, about 15 minutes.
