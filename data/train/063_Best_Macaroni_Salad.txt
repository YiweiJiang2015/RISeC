1) In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under cold water and drain.
2) In a large bowl, combine the pasta, mayonnaise and mix well.
3) Stir in cucumber, onion, ham and cheese.
4) Mix well.
5) Drizzle oil and vinegar over top and toss.
6) Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
