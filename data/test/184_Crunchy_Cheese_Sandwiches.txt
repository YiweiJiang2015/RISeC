1) Preheat your oven's broiler.
2) In a medium bowl, stir together the onion, tomato, celery, bell pepper, cheese, chili powder and salt.
3) Arrange slices of bread on a baking sheet or broiling pan.
4) Top each one with 1/4 of the mixture, spreading out to the edges.
5) Broil for 4 to 5 minutes, or until cheese melts.
6) Watch closely to prevent burning.
7) Serve immediately.
