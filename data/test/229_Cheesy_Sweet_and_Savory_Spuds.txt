1) Place the potatoes and sweet potatoes in a large pot with enough water to cover.
2) Bring to a boil.
3) Remove from heat, and drain.
4) Preheat the oven broiler.
5) In a large bowl, mash the potatoes.
6) Gradually mix in butter, heavy cream, chicken bouillon, brown sugar, and pepper.
7) Transfer the mixture to a medium baking dish, and top with Monterey Jack cheese.
8) Broil uncovered in the preheated oven 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
