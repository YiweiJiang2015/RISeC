1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F ( 175 degrees C).
2) Lightly grease a 9 inch springform pan.
3) In a small bowl, mix together 1 1/2 cups chocolate wafer crumbs, 2 tablespoons sugar, and melted butter.
4) Press onto the bottom and 1 1/2 inch up the sides of the prepared 9 inch springform pan.
5) Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 10 minutes.
6) Allow to cool.
7) Reduce oven temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
8) To Make Filling: In a small saucepan, heat 1/4 cup whipping cream and 1/4 cup chocolate chips, stirring constantly, until chips are melted.
9) In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and 1 cup of sugar until smooth.
10) Add cocoa and beat well.
11) Add eggs and beat on low until just blended.
12) Stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla and reserved chocolate mixture until blended.
13) Pour over crust.
14) Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 45 to 50 minutes or until center is almost set.
15) To Make Topping: In a small saucepan, heat 1/4 cup whipping cream and 1 teaspoon vanilla until just before boiling.
16) Stir constantly and be careful not to boil over.
17) Have 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips ready in a small bowl.
18) Pour in the hot cream and stir with a spoon until smooth.
19) Spread over baked cheesecake and refrigerate overnight.
