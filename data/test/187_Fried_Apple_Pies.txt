1) Peel and dice apples.
2) Add sugar and cinnamon.
3) Cook in a saucepan on low heat.
4) Cook until soft, then mash with fork to form a thick applesauce.
5) Sift flour and salt together.
6) Cut in the shortening.
7) Add water and mix with fork.
8) Roll out to about 1/8 inch thick on a floured board.
9) Cut with a large cookie cutter (4 inches in diameter).
10) In each round, place 1 heaping tablespoon fruit.
11) Moisten edges with cold water, fold and press edge with a fork.
12) Heat oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat.
13) Fry pies, a few at a time, 2 to 3 minutes on each side; cook until golden brown.
14) Drain on paper towels.
15) Sprinkle with confectioners sugar.
