1) In a mixing bowl, combine the first four ingredients.
2) In a saucepan, combine water, oil, butter and cocoa; bring to a boil over medium heat.
3) pour over dry ingredients; mix well.
4) Add eggs, buttermilk, vanilla and baking soda; mix well.
5) Pour into a greased and floured 15-in pan.
6) Bake at 375 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.
7) Meanwhile, for frosting, combine the butter, cream, cocoa and cinnamon in a saucepan.
8) Cook and stir over medium heat until butter is melted and mixture is heated through.
9) Remove from the heat; beat in sugar and vanilla until smooth.
10) Stir in walnuts.
11) Carefully spread over hot cake.
12) Cool completely.
