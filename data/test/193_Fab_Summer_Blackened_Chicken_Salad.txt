1) Warm 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2) Season both sides of chicken with Cajun seasoning, and place in hot oil.
3) Stir in crushed garlic.
4) Cook chicken 5 minutes on each side, or until juices run clear.
5) Remove chicken from skillet, drain on paper towels, cool slightly, and dice; discard oil and garlic.
6) In a small bowl, mix remaining olive oil, balsamic vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper.
7) In a large bowl, gently toss the dressing with the mixed salad greens, arugula, avocado, sun-dried tomatoes, and olives.
8) Top with diced chicken to serve.
