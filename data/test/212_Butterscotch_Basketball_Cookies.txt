1) In a microwave, melt butterscotch chips; cool for 10 minutes.
2) In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars.
3) Add egg, milk and vanilla; mix well.
4) Beat in melted chips.
5) Gradually add flour; mix well.
6) Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.
7) On a floured surface, roll out dough to 1/4-in. thickness.
8) Cut with a floured 4-1/2-in. gingerbread man cookie cutter and a 3-in. round cutter.
9) Place 2 in. apart on greased baking sheets.
10) Bake at 375 degrees F for 5-8 minutes or until edges are lightly browned.
11) Cool for 1 minute; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
12) For frosting, combine shortening, water, flour and vanilla in a mixing bowl; mix well.
13) gradually beat in sugar.
14) Place 1 cup frosting in a plastic bag; cut a small hole in corner of bag.
15) Pipe shirt and shorts on players.
16) Fill in outline and smooth with a metal spatula.
17) Tint 1/4 cup frosting black; place in a plastic bag.
18) Pipe lines on round cookies to create basketballs; pipe hair, eyes and noses on players.
19) Tint 1/4 cup frosting red; pipe a mouth on each player.
20) Tint remaining frosting to match team colors of your choice; pipe around shirts and shorts and add a letter on shirts if desired.
