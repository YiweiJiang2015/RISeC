1) Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2) Drain escargots; rinse in cold water and drain again.
3) In a mixing bowl, combine the butter, garlic, green onion, parsley, pepper, nutmeg and wine.
4) Mix thoroughly.
5) Place 1 teaspoon of butter mixture into each pastry shell.
6) Top each with 1 escargot, cover with another teaspoon of butter mixture and place mushroom cap on top.
7) Arrange pastry shells on a 10x15 inch baking sheet.
8) Bake in a preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes or until golden brown.
