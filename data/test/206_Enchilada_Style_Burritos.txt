1) Place frozen burritos in a greased 13-in baking dish; set aside.
2) In a saucepan, melt butter.
3) Stir in flour until smooth; gradually stir in water.
4) Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
5) Add the tomato sauce, chili powder, bouillon, cumin and salt.
6) Simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes or until thickened.
7) Pour over the burritos.
8) Sprinkle with cheese, olives if desired and onions.
9) Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until heated through.
