1) In a bowl, combine the eggs, crackers, celery and pimientos.
2) In another bowl, combine the mayonnaise, milk, salt, garlic salt and pepper; mix well.
3) Stir into the egg mixture.
4) Spoon about 1/3 cup onto each muffin half; place on an ungreased baking sheet.
5) Broil for 3-4 minutes or until lightly browned.
