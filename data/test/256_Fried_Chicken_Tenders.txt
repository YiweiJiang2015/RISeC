1) Place flour in a shallow bowl.
2) Place breadcrumbs in a separate shallow bowl, and mix with ground black pepper and cayenne pepper.
3) Place eggs and water in a small bowl.
4) One piece at a time, coat the chicken in the flour, the eggs and the bread crumbs, and set aside.
5) Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
6) In small batches, fry chicken 6 to 8 minutes, or until golden brown.
7) In a separate small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, horseradish, sour cream, Worcestershire sauce and mustard.
