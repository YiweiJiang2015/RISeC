1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2) Slice the top off of each bun, saving the tops.
3) Hollow out each bun, leaving a shell.
4) In a medium bowl combine chicken, cream cheese, Pepper Jack cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic and spinach.
5) Spoon mixture into each bun, packing it tightly; cap with bun tops.
6) Wrap each bun tightly in foil.
7) Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
