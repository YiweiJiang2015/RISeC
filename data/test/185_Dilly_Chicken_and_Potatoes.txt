1) In a large skillet over medium heat, brown chicken and potatoes in oil for 10-15 minutes.
2) Remove chicken and potatoes; set aside.
3) Discard all but 1 tablespoon drippings.
4) To drippings, add cream, salt and pepper; stir to mix.
5) Return chicken and potatoes to skillet.
6) Sprinkle with onions and dill.
7) Cover and simmer for 50-60 minutes or until chicken juices run clear and potatoes are tender.
8) With a slotted spoon, remove chicken and potatoes to a serving platter; keep warm.
9) Add sour cream to pan; stir to mix and heat through.
10) Serve with chicken and potatoes.
