1) In a large mixing bowl, cream the shortening and sugar.
2) Beat in the egg and molasses.
3) Combine flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture.
4) Roll teaspoonfuls of dough into balls.
5) Dip one side of each ball into sugar; place with sugar side up on a greased baking sheet.
6) Bake at 350 degrees F for 12-15 minutes or until lightly browned and crinkly.
