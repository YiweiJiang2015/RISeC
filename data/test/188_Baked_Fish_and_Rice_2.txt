1) In a greased 11-in. baking dish, combine the broth, rice, Italian seasoning and garlic powder.
2) Cover and bake at 375 degrees F for 10 minutes.
3) Add the broccoli, Parmesan cheese and half of the onions.
4) Top with fish fillets; sprinkle with paprika.
5) Cover and bake 20-25 minutes longer or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
6) Uncover; sprinkle with cheddar cheese and remaining onions.
7) Return to the oven for 3 minutes or until cheese is melted.
