1) Beat together cream cheese and margarine until well blended.
2) Add flour, mixing well.
3) Shape into a ball, cover and chill for 2 hours.
4) In saucepan over medium heat, cook sugar and milk, stirring occasionally until milk boils and sugar is dissolved.
5) Add the ground nuts.
6) Remove from heat and let cool until mixture is of spreading consistency.
7) Roll out chilled dough on lightly floured surface to a rectangle about 18 X 12 inches.
8) Cut in half lengthwise.
9) Spread each piece with milk/nut filling and roll up like a jelly-roll, starting from the long side.
10) Cut each roll in half.
11) Place seam side down on ungreased cookie sheets (two on a baking sheet).
12) Leaving roll's shape intact, cut into slices 1/2 inch thick almost to the bottom of the roll.
13) Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 15 to 20 minutes or till done.
14) Remove from sheet and sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.
15) Cool on racks.
16) Cut cookies all the way through.
17) Refrigerate or freeze.
18) Resprinkle with confectioners' sugar before serving.
