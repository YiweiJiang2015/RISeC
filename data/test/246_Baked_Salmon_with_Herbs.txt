1) Line a 15-in baking pan with foil; coat foil with nonstick cooking spray.
2) In a bowl, combine the bread crumbs, garlic, Parmesan cheese, parsley, thyme, rosemary, salt and pepper.
3) Add butter; toss to coat evenly.
4) Place salmon on prepared pan.
5) Spray with nonstick cooking spray and pat with bread crumb mixture.
6) Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
