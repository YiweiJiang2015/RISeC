1) Melt 1/4 cup of butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
2) Saute green onions in butter until tender.
3) Remove from pan, and set aside.
4) In the same skillet, melt 1/2 cup of butter, and saute the crawfish for 5 minutes; set aside.
5) In a large pot over medium heat, combine potato soup, mushroom soup, corn, and cream cheese.
6) Mix well, and bring to a slow boil.
7) Stir in half-and-half, sauteed green onions, and crawfish.
8) Season with cayenne pepper.
9) Bring to a low boil, and simmer 5 minutes to blend flavors.
