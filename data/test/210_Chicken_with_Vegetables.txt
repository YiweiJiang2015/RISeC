1) Place mushrooms and chicken in a slow cooker.
2) Add the celery, zucchini, carrots, onion, tomato juice, broth, garlic, paprika and pepper.
3) Cover and cook on low for 5 hours or until meat juices run clear.
4) Remove chicken and vegetables and keep warm.
5) Transfer cooking juices to a saucepan; skim fat.
6) Combine the cornstarch and water until smooth; add to the juices.
7) Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
8) Pour over chicken and vegetables; serve over rice.
