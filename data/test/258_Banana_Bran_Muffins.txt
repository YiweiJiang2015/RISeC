1) Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2) Grease a muffin pan or line with paper muffin liners.
3) In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and brown sugar together until fluffy.
4) Add bananas, milk, vanilla and eggs; mix well.
5) Stir in flour, bran, baking powder, soda and salt; blend just until moistened.
6) Stir in walnuts.
7) Pour batter into prepared muffin cups.
8) Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 20 to 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
9) Cool in the cups for 5 minutes, then remove muffins and place on a wire rack to cool completely.
