1) Measure flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and chocolate chips into a large bowl.
2) Mix thoroughly, and make a well in the center.
3) Beat the egg in a small bowl until frothy.
4) Mix in cooking oil, milk, and bananas.
5) Pour mixture into the well.
6) Stir only to moisten.
7) Fill greased muffin cups 3/4 full.
8) Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 20 to 25 minutes.
