1) In a large skillet or deep fryer, fry chicken wings in oil until done, about 10 minutes.
2) Remove chicken and oil from skillet, drain oil and reserve chicken.
3) Melt butter in skillet, then add vinegar and hot sauce.
4) Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5) Add cooked chicken to sauce and stir over low heat to coat.
6) The longer the wings simmer in the sauce, the hotter they will be.
7) Serve warm.
