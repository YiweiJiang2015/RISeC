1) In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt.
2) In a small mixing bowl, beat egg yolks on low while adding cream.
3) Beat for 1 minute.
4) Add to flour mixture; combine on low speed, then beat on medium-high until smooth.
5) In another small mixing bowl and with clean beaters, beat egg whites on high until stiff peaks form.
6) Gently fold into batter.
7) Bake in a preheated waffle iron according to manufacturer's directions.
8) Serve with warm maple syrup or fresh fruit in season.
