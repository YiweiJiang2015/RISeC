1) In a medium bowl combine apples, blueberries, sugar substitute, and the 1/4 teaspoon apple pie spice; set aside.
2) In another medium bowl stir together whole wheat pastry flour, kosher salt, and the 1/8 teaspoon apple pie spice.
3) Add oil and milk all at once to flour mixture.
4) Stir lightly with a fork until dough comes together.
5) Divide dough into 6 portions.
6) Using six 3 1/2- to 4-inch individual tart pans with removable bottoms, press one dough portion on the bottom and up the side of each tart pan.
7) Divide fruit mixture among pastry-lined pans.
8) Place tart pans on a large baking sheet.
9) Place a large piece of foil over tart pans; press down lightly around the edges to seal.
10) Bake in a 375 degrees F oven for 20 minutes; remove foil.
11) Bake about 20 minutes more or until fruit is tender.
12) Let cool in tart pans on a wire rack for 15 minutes.
13) Carefully remove sides of tart pans.
14) Serve warm or cool.
