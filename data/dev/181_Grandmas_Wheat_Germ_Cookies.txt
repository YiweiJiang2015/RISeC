1) In a large mixing bowl, combine the first six ingredients.
2) Combine the applesauce, oil and eggs.
3) Add to the flour mixture; mix well.
4) Stir in the chocolate chip and walnuts.
5) Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls 2 in apart onto baking sheets coated with nonstick cooking spray.
6) Flatten slightly with a glass bottom coated with nonstick cooking spray.
7) Bake at 350 degrees F for 10-12 minutes or until edges are lightly browned and cookies are set.
8) Cool for 1 minute before removing to wire racks.
