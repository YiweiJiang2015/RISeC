1) Place shortbread cookies on a baking sheet or tray.
2) In a small saucepan over medium-low heat, melt caramels in water, stirring frequently.
3) Spoon a thin line of caramel over each cookie.
4) Place cookies in refrigerator until caramel is set.
5) Line baking sheets or trays with waxed paper.
6) In a double boiler over simmering water, melt chocolate.
7) Dip cookies in chocolate one at a time, tapping against the side of the bowl to remove excess.
8) Place on prepared sheets and let rest at room temperature several hours, until set.
