1) Bring water to boil in a large pot.
2) Place beef shank in, and simmer 1 1/2 hours.
3) Let cool overnight.
4) Lift meat out, trim off gristle and cut meat into medium sized pieces; set aside.
5) Skim fat from surface of stock, or strain through a fine sieve.
6) Return stock to heat, and bring to a boil.
7) Add onions, carrots and rutabaga.
8) Season with salt and pepper.
9) Simmer for 1 hour.
10) Add potatoes, and simmer until tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
11) Stir in the leeks, cabbage, parsley and reserved meat.
12) Simmer 10 minutes, or until cabbage is tender.
