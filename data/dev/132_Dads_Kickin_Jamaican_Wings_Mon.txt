1) Place the jerk seasoning, oil, garlic, ginger, green onions, and jalapeno slices in a blender, and blend until thick and smooth.
2) Dunk wings in the mixture, and arrange in single layer on baking sheets.
3) Cover with aluminum foil, and refrigerate 8 hours, or overnight.
4) Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
5) Bake wings 2 hours in the preheated oven.
