1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2) Place bacon in a skillet and cook over medium heat until the pieces are browned.
3) Remove with a slotted spoon to paper towels.
4) Discard all but 1 tablespoon of bacon grease.
5) Stir in onions and mushrooms, and cook until soft.
6) Remove from heat.
7) In a large bowl, stir together beef, egg, and milk.
8) Stir in the onion and mushrooms.
9) Mix in about 4 ounces of Swiss cheese, and all but 1 tablespoon of bacon.
10) Stir in cornflake crumbs, and mix until well blended.
11) Shape into a loaf, and place in a meatloaf pan.
12) Bake in a preheated oven for 1 hour.
13) Drain fat, and sprinkle with remaining cheese and bacon.
14) Return to oven, and bake until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
