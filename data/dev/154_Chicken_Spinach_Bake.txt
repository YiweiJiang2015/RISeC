1) 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
2) 6 tablespoons butter or margarine, divided
3) Drain and squeeze out excess moisture from spinach.
4) Beat eggs, onion salt and nutmeg.
5) Add spinach and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese; mix well.
6) Combine bread crumbs and remaining Parmesan.
7) Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper; coat with crumb mixture.
8) Place in two greased 13-in baking pans.
9) Spread 2 tablespoons spinach mixture onto each breast.
10) Sprinkle with remaining crumb mixture; drizzle with butter.
11) Bake at 350 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until chicken juices run clear.
12) For sauce, melt 4 tablespoons butter; blend in flour and salt.
13) Stir to form a smooth paste.
14) Add milk; cook and stir until thickened and bubbly.
15) Add cheese and stir until melted.
16) Saute mushrooms in remaining butter.
17) Stir into cheese mixture.
18) Carefully pour sauce over chicken, or pour into a serving bowl and pass.
