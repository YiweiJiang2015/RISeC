1) Place whole potatoes in their skins into a large pot of boiling water; boil for 25 to 30 minutes.
2) Remove potatoes, and discard water.
3) When cool enough to handle, peel potatoes, and place on a lightly floured surface.
4) Mash potatoes with a rolling pin.
5) Place mashed potatoes into a large bowl.
6) Stir in flour, egg, parsley, salt, and nutmeg.
7) Knead well to form a smooth dough.
8) Then roll out the dough to a thickness of about 1/2 inch.
9) Cut flattened dough into thin strips, about 1 1/2 inches long.
10) Gently roll out the strips, or stretch them until the ends taper.
11) Set aside for 15 minutes.
12) In a large skillet, heat lard over medium heat.
13) Place the potato strips into the skillet, and fry until golden brown on both sides.
