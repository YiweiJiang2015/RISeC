1) In a large skillet over medium heat, combine chicken, lemonade, olive oil, lime juice, and Worcestershire sauce.
2) Season with garlic powder, onion powder, and bay leaf.
3) Simmer until chicken is no longer pink, and juices run clear, 15 to 20 minutes.
4) Meanwhile, warm the tortillas in the oven or microwave until soft.
5) When chicken is fully cooked, transfer to serving bowl.
6) Place lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, salsa, and sour cream in serving dishes.
7) Each person can create their own wrap, using their preferred ingredients.
