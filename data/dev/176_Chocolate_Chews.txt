1) Melt the chocolate chips in a heavy saucepan over very low heat.
2) Stir to evenly melt.
3) Cream together the butter and sugars until light.
4) Beat in the eggs, one at a time.
5) Stir in the melted chocolate and vanilla.
6) In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder and baking soda.
7) Add to the butter mixture slowly.
8) Blend thoroughly.
9) Shape into 1-inch balls and place on a greased cookie sheet.
10) Bake at 350 degrees F (180 degrees C) for 12 to 14 minutes.
11) Let sit on cookie sheet for a minute or two before removing to a rack to cool.
