1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2) Prepare stuffing according to package directions.
3) Wash the chicken, pat dry and pound out to about a 1/2 inch thickness.
4) Place a slice of cheese on each breast, followed by a scoop of prepared stuffing.
5) Place stuffed breasts in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
6) Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 minutes, or until chicken juices run clear, then place a second slice of cheese over each stuffed chicken, and bake for 2 more minutes, or until cheese is bubbly.
