1) Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2) Thoroughly cream together shortening, sugars, eggs and vanilla.
3) In a separate bowl, mix together the dry ingredients, except the candies.
4) Add to creamed mixture and mix well.
5) Stir in 1/2 cup candies.
6) Mix well.
7) Drop by teaspoon onto cookie sheets and decorate with remaining candies.
8) Bake for 10 - 12 minutes.
