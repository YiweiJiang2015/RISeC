1) Preheat an oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2) Grease cookie sheets.
3) In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt butter till lightly browned.
4) Pour into medium sized bowl.
5) Stir in the egg white, sugar and vanilla.
6) Combine the flour cocoa, and salt; mix into the butter mixture.
7) Fold in chocolate.
8) Drop by teaspoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto cookie sheets.
9) Bake for 12 to 15 minutes.
10) Transfer to rack to cool and dust with confectioners' sugar.
