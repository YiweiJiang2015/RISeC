1) Mix together flour, salt and baking powder.
2) Add 3/4 cup butter or margarine (do not use diet Magarine).
3) Mix in 2 eggs, slightly beaten and 2 teaspoons vanilla.
4) Spread batter into a sprayed 9 x 13 inch metal pan.
5) Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 25 minutes.
6) Cool 15 minutes in baking pan.
7) Cut brownies into 1 1/2 inch squares.
8) Cool completely in pan.
9) Make 2 dozen brownies.
