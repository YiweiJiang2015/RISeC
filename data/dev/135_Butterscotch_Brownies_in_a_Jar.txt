1) Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2) Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
3) Empty jar of brownie mix into a large mixing bowl; stir to break up lumps.
4) Add 3/4 cup softened butter, 2 beaten eggs, and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract; mix until well blended.
5) Spread batter evenly in the prepared pan.
6) Bake for 25 minutes.
7) Allow to cool in the pan some before cutting into squares.
