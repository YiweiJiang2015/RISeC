1) Combine brown sugar, 2/3 cup shortening, water, and 1 teaspoon vanilla in large bowl.
2) Beat eggs into creamed mixture.
3) In another bowl, combine flour, cocoa, salt, and baking soda.
4) Mix this mixture into creamed mixture until just blended.
5) Stir in chocolate chips.
6) Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheet about 2 inches apart.
7) Place cookie sheet in the middle of the oven.
8) Cool for 2 minutes on baking sheet.
9) Remove cookies to wire rack to cool completely.
10) To Make Frosting: Begin by combining evaporated milk, white sugar, 1/4 cup shortening, and egg yolks in medium saucepan.
11) Stir 1/2 teaspoon vanilla, pecans, and coconut into the warm mixture.
12) Cool completely, and frost cooled cookies.
