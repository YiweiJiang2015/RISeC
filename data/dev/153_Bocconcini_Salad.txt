1) In a large salad bowl, combine the mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, bell pepper, celery, endive and arugula.
2) Whisk together the lemon juice and olive oil; pour over the salad and season to taste with salt and pepper.
3) Toss until all the ingredients are thoroughly coated with the dressing.
4) Sprinkle the basil over the salad and serve immediately.
